Project

After working for more than a year as a data scientist at bunq, in August 2021, I have started the Master of Engineering in Operations Research and Information Engineering at Cornell Tech. This master degree is based in NYC.

Domain

Study

These are the courses that I will be taking during my first semester:

  • CS 5112 Algorithms and Data Structures for Applications
  • CS 5781 Machine Learning Engineering
  • CS 5785 Applied Machine Learning
  • ORIE 5380 Optimization Methods
  • ORIE 5530 Modeling Under Uncertainty
  • NBAY 5301 Intro to Entreprenurial Finance
  • TECH 5900 Product Studio
    •

    Most of the courses in this list are Computer and Data Science oriented. On the other hand, Product Studio is a highly applied course in which a team of 5 students (1 ORIE, 2 CS, 2 MBA) is working together to create a technological product that would solve a challenge provided by a certain company. In my case, the company is Datadog, whereas the challenge is "How might we help sales teams adapt to rapidly changing product offerings?"
