I started out at bunq as a Data Science Intern. After 4 months, I was offered to continue my contract as a part-time Data Scientist (3-4 days a week). In this position, I worked for about a year.

The first project that I worked on was building a Customer Lifetime Value model (CLV). Essentially, this was an end-to-end ML project which involved all the steps starting from Data Wrangling and Model Training to Model Deployment and Continuous Performance Monitoring.

CLV model has 2 use cases:

  1. Aggregate level: Analytics

    • Allows understanding the marketing campaign quality in terms of estimated profits already 2 days after the start (with up to 10% error). After 14 days, the mean absolute error drops to 5%. Previously, recognizing customers' value was only possible after ~3 months of their signup.

    • Helps to identify ad fatigue as early as possible.

    • Led to 27% increase in ROI (Return on investment) of our marketing campaigns.

  2. User level: Targetting the best customers

    • Allows bunq to imitate custom events which trigger in case the user reaches a certain threshold in CLV. These events are then shared with Google and other Ad Managers to improve the targetting performance of bunq's marketing campaigns.

    • Led to ~40% increase in ROI of Google Ads marketing campaigns.
