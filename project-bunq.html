I started out at bunq as a Data Science Intern. After 4 months, I was offered to continue my contract as a part-time Data Scientist (3-4 days a week). In this position, I worked for about a year.

During my time at bunq, I had a chance to work on many exciting projects!

The first project that I did was to build a Customer Lifetime Value model (CLV). Essnetially, this was an end-to-end ML project which involved everything from Data Wrangling to Model Training and Evaluation to Model Deployment and Continuous Performance Monitoring.

CLV model has 2 use cases:

  1. Analytics -> aggregate level
    • Allows understanding the marketing campaign quality in terms of $$$ already 2 days after the start (with 10% error). Previously, it was only clear after ~3 months.
    • Helps to identify ad fatigue as early as possible.
    • Led to 30% increase in ROI (Return on investment) of our marketing campaigns.
  2. Targetting best customers -> user level
    • Allows us to generate custom events which trigger in case the user reaches a certain threshold in CLV. These events are then shared with Google and other Ad Managers to improve the targetting performance of bunq's marketing campaigns.
    • Led to ~40% increase in ROI of Google Ads marketing campaigns.
